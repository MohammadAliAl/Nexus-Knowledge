Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Nexus Knowledge is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information of both students and tutors.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Aadhar number (for identification purposes)

This information is collected from both students and tutors to ensure proper identification and qualification verification.

3. Use of Data

We use the collected data for:

  • Matching students with suitable tutors
  • Ensuring compliance with legal requirements
  • Record-keeping for future reference in case of disputes

4. Data Sharing

We do not share personal information with any third parties. All the information collected remains within Nexus Knowledge and is stored securely.

5. Data Security

Your data is stored in Google Cloud, which is equipped with industry-standard encryption and security protocols. We ensure that the information is protected from unauthorized access.

6. Cookies

Nexus Knowledge does not use cookies on its website, ensuring that your browsing experience remains private.

7. Children's Privacy

For students under 18, their personal information is typically provided by their parents or guardians. We ensure that all interactions involving minors are conducted through responsible adults.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated to users through appropriate channels.
